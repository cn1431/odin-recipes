Salad

Description

Quick and easy mediterranean salad that is healthy and delcious!

Ingredients

For the salad

  • 2 can chickpeas, drained, rinsed
  • 1 medium cucumber, chopped
  • 1 capscium, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly slices
  • 1/2 cup chopped olives
  • 1/2 crumbed feta
  • Salt
  • Pepper

For the lemon-parsly vinaigrette

  • 1/2 cup extra virgin oil
  • 1/4 cup white wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp freshc chopped parsly
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, toss together chickpeas, cucumber, bell pepper, red onion, olives, and feta. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. In a jar fitted with a lid, combine olive oil, vinegar,lemon juice, parsley, and red pepper flakes. Close the jar and shake until emulsified, then season with salt and pepper.
  3. Dress salad with vinaigrette just before serving.
